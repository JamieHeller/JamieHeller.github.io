Edmonds-Karp Algorithm

Usage Details

    Usage Details:

  • Adding a node: Click in the open space to add a node
  • Adding an edge: Drag from a source node to a target node.
  • Selecting a node: Perform a left mouse click on that node.
  • Selecting an edge: Perform a left mouse click on that edge.
  • Deleting a node:
    • Method 1: Perform a left mouse click on that node and press backspace or delete.
    • Method 2: Perform a left mouse clock on that node and press the delete button.
  • Deleting an edge:
    • Method 1: Perform a left mouse click on that edge and press backspace or delete.
    • Method 2: Perform a left mouse clock on that edge and press the delete button.
  • Moving a node:
    • Method 1: Press the alt-key with a left mouse click and move the node in the graph.
    • Method 2: Check the Enable Move box and with a left mouse click, move the node in the graph.
  • Change a Node's name: Click on a node to select it. Type a new value in the value text box and press enter.
  • Change a Link's capacity: Click on a link to select it. Type a new capacity value in the value text box and press enter.
